Projects

Design of a Biomedical Instrument using MATLAB and Simulink

- Designed and Simulated the working of an Electronic Stethoscope in a team of 3.

- Evaluated the performance of various components via literature survey and made design matrix.

- Used Simulink for designing the circuit of signal acquisition and conditioning and simulating the results.

- Project done as a part of the course 'Biosensing and Bioinstrumentation' under the mentorship of Dr. Srikanth Sugavanam.

Project

Biostatistical Data Analysis using R

- Analysed the dataset of a controlled experiment using two-way ANOVA and linear regression, in which the cholesterol level LDL was measured.

- Conducted post-hoc test and obtained the scatter plot of residuals to investigate two factors: effect of (i) medical dugs and (ii) lifestyle

- Project done as a part of the course 'Applied Biostatistics' under the mentorship of Dr. Erwin Fuhrer.
